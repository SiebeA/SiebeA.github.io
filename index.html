The Greek alphabet is different from the Latin alphabet, used by e.g. the Germanic (English, German) or Romance languages (Spanish, French)..

The additional step of learning a new Alphabet is often a hurdle for language learners

This website, therefore, is aimed at teaching you to interpet and use the Greek aphabet in the shortest amount of time possible.

(less than 5 attention-hours ideally spread out over a couple of days)

The resources on this website are exactly configured for the purpose of getting from 0 to A1 level

NOTE: Install Greek language keyboard on your PC; for Windows: "Time & Language > Add Language" (also, checkmark the TTS capacity for the proper nouns page)

The links are ordered; the completion of the former facilitates the next.
