The Greek alphabet is different from either Germanic (English, Germian, etc) or latin languages (Spanish, French) This is often a hurdle for language learners.

This website, therefore, is aimed at teaching you to interpet and use the Greek aphabet in the shortest amount of time possible (less than 5 attention-hours ideally spread out over a couple of days)

The resources on this website are exactly configured for the purpose of getting from 0 to A1 level; and it is my assertion that no other website serves that particular purpose as effective and efficient; as it was exactly developed for that purpose in exact conjunction with my personal practical requirement of learning the Greek language.

NOTE: Install Greek language keyboard on your PC; for Windows: "Time & Language > Add Language" (also, checkmark the TTS capacity for the proper nouns page)
